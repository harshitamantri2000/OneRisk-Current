1.  Executive  Summary  
The  Risk  Triangulation  Engine  (RTE)  is  an  AI-powered  financial  intelligence  platform

Problem  Statement:  Indian  credit  underwriters  evaluate  ITR,  GST,  MCA,  and  bank  statements  in  isolation  —  a  fragmented  approach  that  allows  material  misrepresentations  to  go  undetected.  A  company  may  declare  ₹4.12  Cr  PAT  in  ITR  while  its  bank  statement  reveals  ₹0  in  vendor  payments,  or  claim  zero  related-party  transactions  in  AS-18  while  BSA  shows  ₹3.77  Cr  received  from  the  holding  company.  RTE  detects  these  contradictions  automatically.  
 
Product  Vision:  To  become  the  definitive  pre-sanction  intelligence  layer  for  fintech  lenders  and  NBFCs  in  India  —  delivering  forensic-grade  risk  analysis  in  hours,  not  days.

8.  Glossary  
Term  Definition  
GSTR-3B  Monthly  GST  return  filed  by  a  registered  taxpayer  declaring  outward  supplies,  ITC,  and  tax  payments  
GSTR-2B  Auto-generated  input  tax  credit  statement  for  the  buyer,  based  on  supplier  GSTR-1  filings  
GSTR-1  Monthly/quarterly  return  filed  by  a  supplier  declaring  outward  supplies  (invoices  issued)  
ITR  Income  Tax  Return  form  for  companies  other  than  those  claiming  exemption  under  Section  11  
AOC-4  MCA  form  for  filing  financial  statements  (balance  sheet,  P&L,  cash  flow)  with  the  Registrar  of  Companies  
ITC  Input  Tax  Credit  —  GST  paid  on  inputs,  deductible  against  output  tax  liability  
RCM  Reverse  Charge  Mechanism  —  GST  payable  by  the  recipient,  not  the  supplier  (applies  to  certain  services  like  imports)  
AS-18  Accounting  Standard  18  —  Related  Party  Disclosures  (mandatory  for  companies  filing  with  MCA)  
DPO  Days  Payable  Outstanding  —  average  number  of  days  a  company  takes  to  pay  its  vendors  
DSO  Days  Sales  Outstanding  —  average  number  of  days  to  collect  payment  after  a  sale  is  made  
DSCR  Debt  Service  Coverage  Ratio  —  EBITDA  /  (Interest  +  Principal)  —  measures  ability  to  service  debt  
CAM  Credit  Assessment  Memo  —  structured  document  summarising  all  financial  and  risk  analysis  for  a  credit  decision  
BSA  Bank  Statement  Analysis  —  systematic  review  and  categorisation  of  bank  transactions  
NPA  Non-Performing  Asset  —  loan  where  principal  or  interest  is  overdue  by  90+  days  
Section  16(4)  
Provision  of  CGST  Act  that  bars  ITC  on  invoices  not  filed  within  the  statutory  time  limit  
Rule  37A  (GST)  
Requires  reversal  of  ITC  if  vendor  has  not  paid  corresponding  tax  within  180  days  of  invoice  
Triangulation  
Cross-validation  of  the  same  financial  parameter  (e.g.,  revenue)  across  ITR,  GST,  MCA,  and  BSA  to  detect  inconsistencies
